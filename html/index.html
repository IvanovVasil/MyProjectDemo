Welcome to the official website of volleyball club “Popovo 09". Here you can read more about VC “Popovo 09”, the club, its team and ambitions.

Volleyball Club "Popovo 09" is the men's volleyball team of the town of Popovo, which is the successor of VC "Popovo". Competitors from the club compete in the northern "A" elite volleyball group of Bulgaria Chairman of the club is Todor Raichev from Targovishte. In 1998, on the initiative of the Municipality of Popovo, VC "Popovo 09" was established, which trains children and adolescents up to 18 * years of age, with no restrictions for boys and girls.
